Interior & Exterior Design for Residential and Commercial Buildings


Sparkle & Stone Building Consultants is a full-service company located in the Kollam area since 2001, specializing in both residential and commercial building interior & exterior design & construction.
Whether you need a quick refresh or an extensive whole renovation, we can provide the expertise to make sure the project runs smoothly and gives you the best results possible. Because we are a small firm, we are extremely flexible and nimble. We have our own expert team for fit outs & have wide relationships with many local craftsmen and subcontractors, so you can be assured that your project is completed with the highest standards.
